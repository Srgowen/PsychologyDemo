About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

With over 15 years of experience in clinical psychology, Dr. Sarah Mitchell is dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based mental health care. She specializes in anxiety disorders, depression, trauma recovery, and relationship counseling.

Dr. Mitchell holds a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from Stanford University and is licensed in multiple states. She has published numerous research papers on cognitive-behavioral therapy and trauma treatment, and regularly speaks at professional conferences.

Her approach combines the latest scientific research with genuine empathy and understanding, creating a safe space where clients can explore their challenges and develop effective coping strategies.
